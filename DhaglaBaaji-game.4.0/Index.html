How to Play Dhaglabaaji

1. This is a game between player 1 and player 2.

2. In this game both players get 26 cards initially.

3. Now player 1 put one random card in the middle.

4. Then player 2 will put one card in middle and if both card has same value then All the middle cards are will be given to player 2.

5. Here same value means 2,2 j,j k,k like this.if you put k and playre 2's card is also k then this is the match.

6. If player 2 does not get match then again your turn and this will be continue till there is a match.

7. If one of the player has no card and in middle there is some cards then middle cards will be given to both player equally

8. If any player has no card and in middle also no card is available then other player will win the match.

9. Here when you click start button it will give 26 cards to both. Then when you click turn, your card will be given to you and by clicking done button you are putting that card in middle.

10. If you get the match then turn will be again yours.
